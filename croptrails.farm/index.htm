About us

Let Us Tell You Our Story

CropTrails, a web and android application, is a SaaS model of Innovation based company, OEPP Innovations Pvt Ltd (9930i) which provides ag-tech solutions for worldwide agricultural problems. It is designed to bridge gaps in the process of Farm Management. The tools are used to sustain and increase efficiency and productivity of the yield.

An initiative to salute those who 'work in acres not in hours'.

Solutions

Our Diverse Solutions

We democratize data for all stakeholders of the industry, connecting the ecosystem to a platform that empowers them to benefit from data-driven farming. CropTrails mobile applications are scalable from small co-operatives, farmer producer organization to large agribusiness corporations and government agencies working in the sustainable development sector.

  • Farm Management

    Collect data during Farmer and Farm enrollment, Save and update it periodically, Data helps Government to formulate policies.

  • Farm Inspection

    For pests and crop diseases, Irrigation and fertigation processes, Whether crop calendar is implemented or not.

  • Crop Calendar Implementation

    Input crop calendars, Assign them to farmers and supervisors, Track and verify response of planning.

  • Analytical Dashboard & Reports

    Insightful dashboard, Simplified stats and reports, Quick data retrieval in filtered formats, Customised reports available.

  • Farm Financial Management

    Manage inventory accounts, Manages cost of cultivation, Manage your farm related financial accounts all-in-one place.
